Token Issuance

We provide solutions to handle the entire token issuance process, from the onboarding of investors to the delivery of your tokens. Our regulatory compliant token infrastructure introduces standards and procedures that can be seamlessly utilized by token issuers to ensure that their security tokens comply with worldwide transfer restrictions, thereby opening up a global pool of security token liquidity.

Regulatory Compliant Token Infrastructure

Our platform enables token issuers to ensure that the transfer of security tokens comply with local and global regulatory requirements. We support Regulation S and Regulation D offerings (other options available), to assist you on your journey to creating your security token. From issuing your security token to managing the investors and token distribution via an integrated whitelist system, you will have everything you need to guarantee compliance.
