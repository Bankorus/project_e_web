MetaMask

Bankorus facilitates the ownership and transfer of tokens using the Ethereum blockchain. This means that when a trade settles, the tokens are transferred directly to the buyer's Ethereum address. Bankorus will not have access to these tokens, only the address owner will have access to them. MetaMask is a tool that gives users an easy way to interact with the Ethereum blockchain. It is a bridge that allows you to manage your Ethereum wallet in your browser. MetaMask includes a secure identity vault/wallet, providing a user interface to manage your identities on different sites and sign blockchain transactions.

You can install the MetaMask add-on in Chrome, Firefox, Opera, and the new Brave browser.

MetaMask's mission is to make Ethereum as easy to use for as many people as possible.

Learn how to setup your Metamask account

1You need the MetaMask browser extension in order to use the Bankorus platform.

GET METAMASK

2 Select Your Browser option and add the MetaMask Extension. Once the extension is installed the MetaMask fox icon should now be located in the upper-right corner of your browser.

3Once MetaMask is installed please create a new account (or enter your account seed).

4 Make sure you record your Recovery Seed Phrase somewhere safe! The Recovery Seed is a short string of words created specifically for your account, that you can use to restore your account and recover funds if this computer is lost or stolen. You are the only one who controls your Metamask account. This means that any tokens in a lost account cannot be recovered by Bankorus Network or Metamask. The only way to recover tokens in an account is by utilizing your Recovery Seed.
